# Superstore-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
This project analyzes the sales performance of a global retail company using the Superstore dataset. The goal is to uncover key business insights around revenue, profitability, customer segments, and geographic performance. The analysis is presented through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

**Objectives**:-
1. Analyze overall sales and profit performance
2. Identify top-performing regions and countries
3. Understand category-wise profitability
4. Track monthly sales trends and seasonality
5. Highlight top products contributing to revenue and profit

**Tools & Technologies**:-
1. Power BI → Data visualization & dashboarding
2. Power Query → Data cleaning & transformation
3. DAX → Measures and calculations
4. Excel / CSV → Data source

**Dataset**:-
Source: Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
Records: ~51,000 rows

**Key fields include**:

Order Date
Sales, Profit, Discount
Category, Sub-category
Country, Region
Customer Segment

**Dashboard Features**:-

**KPI Summary**:-
Total Sales: $12.64M
Total Profit: $1.47M
Profit Margin: 11.62%
Total Orders: 51K

**Sales Trend Analysis**:-
Monthly sales trend (2011–2014)
Identifies growth patterns and seasonality

**Geographic Performance**:-
Top 10 countries by sales
Highlights key revenue-driving markets

**Category Profitability**:-
Compares profit across product categories
Identifies high-margin segments

**Product Performance**:-
Top products by profit
Shows key contributors to business success

**Key Insights**:-
1. The United States is the dominant market, contributing the highest share of total sales.
2. Sales show a steady upward trend, with noticeable peaks during Q4 seasonal periods.
3. The Technology category generates the highest profit among all categories.
4. A small number of products contribute significantly to overall profitability.
